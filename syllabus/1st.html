    Student FAQ - Common Questions

    Q: What is a "Non-Gradial" course?

    A: These are courses like NSS, NCC, or Induction (DIKSHA) that are mandatory to attend and pass, but their marks/credits are not added to your final percentage calculation.

    Q: How many practical classes will I have for Poultry Production?

    A: According to the syllabus, there are 32 practical sessions planned covering everything from breed identification to economics.

    Q: What is the ratio of Theory to Practical in Fundamentals of Agronomy?

    A: The course is 3(2+1), meaning you have 2 theory lectures and 1 practical session per week.

    Q: Do I need to study both Math and Biology?

    A: No, the course is listed as MATH/BIO-111, typically depending on your background in higher secondary school.
